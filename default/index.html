Basketball

Quit counting points and looking at the scoreboard. Quit avoiding the real game because of limitations. The only limitation is the conscience; the only barrier is the mind. It is not about the result or your abilities. It is about the exuberance the game gives you. There is no greater award than this feeling. Give in to it. Stop worrying about how WELL you play. It is all about how PASSIONATELY you do it. Bring the passion to the field.

Dodgeball

It’s five versus five. The game is on and you are there, on the court, facing the unbeatable dodgeball players. You’re not the greatest. Neither are you the fittest and most skillful. But what you have is a lion’s heart dying to play out those dodge balls. A heart desperate to avoid each and every rival shot, craving to blast that last throw which would win it all.

Sparta

Power, skill, potential, strength, courage, vigor, toughness, vitality and energy. Make no mistake, this is the SPARTA challenge. The ultimate combination of Powerlifting, Tug-of-War and Armwrestling, a competition that will bring out shouts, lifts and pulls that will make Olympus stand on its feet. These mortals stand beyond that which can be limited, and most importantly, when they fight together, they become entangled in bursting flame of desire that flares so strongly it can change the atmosphere in the room.

Who are we

AUBG Olympics alumni have donated an AUBG Sports Wall of Fame, located in the ABF Student Center. The official opening happened in September 2014. The wall's purpose is for all sports teams at AUBG to have an opportunity to display their sports achievements. The alumni hope for the wall to grow bigger; therefore, they have opened a channel of communication with the Development Office.

Charity

For the day of the main event April 27 AUBG Olympics is preparing a set of fun charity games. Participants will be donating whatever amount of money they find suitable in the form of an entry fee for the games. Apart from taking part in the charity games, anyone can contribute to the initiative by doing a direct donation. The first 30 people to contribute will get a special T-shirt from the AUBG Olympics crew.
